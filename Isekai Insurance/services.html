Races

Races determine one's physical appearance and stats with racial traits. Race and gender is randomly assigned to our customers, however we can assign you to a race and gender of your choice at the cost of 2 sp.
Human
Jacks of all trade and master of none. Humans are th most widepsread race in every world. They do not posses any particular racial trait and usually have stats that are evenly distributed. Lineage has a relatively big impact on one's trait.
Elves
Dwellers of the woods, they specialize in bows and occasionaly spirit magic. Elves are usually taller than the average human and posses long pointy ears as their racials traits. Theirs stats are usually more centered around dexterity and agility at the cost of vitality and strenght. Lineage can make a big difference as pureblooded elfs are called a High Elfs and possses high affinity for spirit magic.
Dwarves
Masters of the earth, they specialize in heavy weaponry and smithing. Dwarves are short but brave and usually stronger than the average human. They posses high vitality and strenght at the cost of intelligence and agility. Lineage has a realatively small impact on one's trait.
Beast people
Beast people look human at first glance but they all posses an animal trait, be it the ears, the tail or any other defining trait of the animal. There are four major subrace for the beast-people, the cat people, the wolfkin, the rabbitkin and dragonkin. They all posses different stats distribution and lineage influence specific to their subrace.

Stats

Stats are what determine one's physical and magical abilities which grows and recedes with one's age. On average 100 points are evenly distributed on birth, but you can distribute your stats however you like for 5 sp. Races will heavily influence stats on birth and stats growth as some are predisposed to gain or lose some of the stats.
