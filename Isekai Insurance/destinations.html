Worlds
Welcome to the destinations page. Currently, there are three available worlds for your reincarnation. One will be randomly assigned to you unless you specify one that you would prefer over the other two. Althought, they are different from the orignal source, all worlds have been modeled to ressemble media from this world. We would suggest you choose one you are familliar with, however if you do not know any of these worlds, you can learn more about them below.

Eorzea

Final Fantasy XIV
Final Fantasy XIV was orignally an mmo, the second mmo out of the long series of rpg "Final Fantasy", developed and published by Square Enix in 2010. It was first released on pc and ps3, but was unfortunately received very poorly. Not giving up, the studio published a revised version of the game that could be considered a whole new game, Final Fantasy XIV: A realm Reborn. Eorzea is the world where the game takes place. It is relatively smaller than the other two worlds however it is more populated and less dangerous than the other two.

Aincrad

Sword Art Online
Sword Art online is orignally a japanese light novel written by Reki Kawahara and illustrated by abec in 2002. The story takes place in the year 2022 where full-dive technology (virtual reality that is completely immmersive) has been created. On November 6, 10000 players would log on Sword Art Online, the first VRMMORPG of its kind, for the first time. It was at that time that they discovered that they could not log out and thus could not go back to their orignal non-virtual world. The game master shows announces that players will die in real life if they died in the game and that the only way out was to beat the 100 floors of Aincrad.

Disboard

No game no life: zero
No game no Life : zero is an anime film based on the light novel series No Game No Life by Yuu Kamiya, more specifically volume 6. The story is a pre-quel to the popular No Game No Life anime. It takes places in the same, a world named Disboard, over 6000 years before the events of the anime, during the Great War. Humans were the weakest of the weak comprarable to the insects fighting agaisnt nuclear warheads of our world, with no capacity for magic and far behind the technology of the dwarves, they could not fight any of the other 15 races. Forced to hide beneath the rubles, scrap at the remaining of the other races and to scarifices themselves for the survival or their race, only 3000 individual remains.
